Hello there! River Studio is my solo-preneur studio for open source software specializing in Python, memes and community.

# Who Am I

My name is River, and River Studio is the frontname I chose for my techical creative work. My main passion is Python and democratized, open source software, and I have been using Python for all kinds of usages from science and schoolwork to professional development for many years now. I lead Python and software education wherever I am and guide others in growing as people and developers, and I can help you too if you'd want me to!

Apart from being a coding girl and educator I am also a local transgender rights and community activist, a musician, and an amature woodworker. If you've seen me around in cons and the likes - I am the girl with the colorful hair :3
